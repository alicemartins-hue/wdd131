About us

At Brazilian Cities Guide, we are passionate about connecting travelers with the heart and soul of Brazil. We believe that travel is not just about visiting new places—it’s about experiencing the culture, flavors, landscapes, and stories that make each city unique.

Our team is dedicated to curating unforgettable journeys across Brazil's most iconic destinations and hidden gems. Whether you're drawn to the vibrant energy of Rio de Janeiro, the historic streets of Salvador, or the breathtaking natural beauty of Lençóis Maranhenses, we are here to guide you every step of the way.

We don't just offer travel services—we offer experiences carefully crafted to help you create lasting memories.

Our History

Brazilian Cities Guide was born from our passion for exploring Brazil beyond the traditional routes. After years of traveling through small towns, big cities, and hidden places, we realized that many travelers were looking for something more authentic.

We created this project to help you experience Brazil in a true and unforgettable way.
